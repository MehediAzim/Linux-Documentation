## 1. How to reduce terminal text / Customizing BASH Prompt

#### Step 1: Small
```console
rafsanjani@mrz:~$ PS1='[\u@\h \W]\$ '
```
#### Step 2: Extra Small
```console
rafsanjani@mrz:~$ PS1='[@ \W]\$ '
```
&nbsp;
&nbsp;

**Note:** The step doesn't mean you have to follow one by one.
